Our Speciality's

Eye Surgery

Eye surgery, also known as ocular surgery, treats vision problems or eye conditions. Procedures include LASIK for vision correction.

Knee Surgery

Knee surgery involves a medical procedure to repair or replace damaged structures in the knee joint, such as cartilage, ligaments, or bone.

Face Surgery

Face surgery, or facial plastic surgery, involves procedures to enhance aesthetics or address medical concerns.

Foot Surgery

Foot surgery addresses deformities, injuries, or chronic conditions affecting the foot. Common procedures include bunion removal, plantar fasciitis release, and fracture repair.

Nose Surgery

Nose surgery, or rhinoplasty, is a medical procedure to reshape or reconstruct the nose for cosmetic or functional reasons

Kidney Surgery

Kidney surgery involves removing or repairing a kidney due to conditions like kidney stones, cancer, injury, or transplant requirements.

Brain Surgery

Brain surgery, or neurosurgery, is a delicate procedure to treat brain disorders such as tumors, aneurysms, trauma, epilepsy, or Parkinson's disease.

Liver Surgery

Liver surgery, or hepatic surgery, involves procedures to treat liver conditions such as tumors, cysts, or liver failure.

Ear Surgery

Ear surgery, also known as otoplasty or otologic surgery, treats structural or functional issues of the ear.

Neck Surgery

Neck surgery addresses issues related to the cervical spine, thyroid, lymph nodes, or other neck structures.

Teeth Surgery

Teeth surgery, also known as oral or dental surgery, addresses dental issues like impacted wisdom teeth, gum disease, or jaw alignment problems.
